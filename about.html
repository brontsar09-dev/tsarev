Our story

We are TSAREV — natural marmalade made without chemistry, without artificial additives, and with real fruit in every spoon.

This wonderful brand began when Daniel, just 11 years old, started turning his idea into something real. He was coming back from boxing training, and one simple moment turned into a bigger dream — a brand built on care, freshness, and natural flavor.

Every jar is made with love, honesty, and the belief that simple ingredients can create something truly special.
